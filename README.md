# Cross-Asset-Correlations
Developed a multivariate DCC‑GARCH model to forecast forward cross‑asset correlations and evaluate regime stability across equities and rates. Produced scenario‑driven correlation surfaces and simulated price paths to assess stress‑period dynamics. Identified a major correlation breakdown between S&P 500 E‑mini futures and 30‑Year U.S. Treasury Bond futures (approximately 0.60 → 0.06) with near‑zero tail dependence (~0.002), indicating a potential structural shift in the equity–rates relationship.
